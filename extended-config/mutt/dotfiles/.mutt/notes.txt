To subscribe to a new list:

1. Subscribe

for google groups email <groupname>+subscribe@googlegroups.com
from here: http://webapps.stackexchange.com/a/15593

2. Change .muttrc

3. Change procmailrc and upload it to webfaction

Webfaction Control Panel > E-MAILS > Mailboxes > ian_inbox

~~~~~
Folder deletions don't propogate from local --> remove, so you have to delete the folders manually through webfaction's email webapp.
